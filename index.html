About me
I am an Ikerbasque tenure-track Researcher at the Basque Center for Applied Mathematics (BCAM), and at Ikerbasque, the Basque Foundation for Science. My research is in the fields of computational stochastics, uncertainty quantification and computational and industrial mathematics, with a focus on multilevel (quasi-) Monte Carlo methods, PDEs with random coefficients, reduced- and mixed-precision numerical algorithms, and biomedical computing.

Interested in doing a PhD or a postdoc in my group?
Drop me an email with your CV!
